Let's Play Rock-Paper-Scissors!

Instructions:

Choose one of the three options ( Rock, Paper or Scissors) and the computer will choose one at the same time.

  • Paper covers rock
  • Rock crushes scissors
  • Scissors cut paper

The winner of each round gets a point. If both players choose the same option then no points are gained. The player with most points at the end of 10 rounds wins the game.
